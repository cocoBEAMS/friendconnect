What is Verification?

Verification is a status granted to select accounts to indicate authenticity and trustworthiness. Verified accounts display a blue checkmark on their profile.

rule

Eligibility Requirements

  • A complete and accurate profile (name, age, and bio)
  • Account in good standing with no abuse history
  • Compliance with FriendConnect Terms and Community Guidelines
  • One verified account per individual
fact_check

Review Process

Verification is reviewed manually by FriendConnect administrators. Approval is discretionary and not guaranteed.

Verification status may be revoked at any time if an account no longer meets eligibility requirements.
lock

Availability

Verification requests are currently limited. Public application may become available in the future.
